Things To Do

1. Ceboruco

A Volcano?! That is right! An active volcano! Check out 1 of the 2 volcanoes in Nayarit. This volcano is very popular with tourists and you can even reach the top! You can see sights such as cinder cones and lava domes. Ceboruco is part of the Trans-Mexican Volcano Belt. Even though it is an active volcano, the last major eruption occured during the 1870s.

2. San Blas

Nothing wrong with a good beach. Come to San Blas and enjoy a nice day at the beach! Whether you enjoy getting into the water or checking out the local shops, San Blas has got you covered! It also a hot bird migratory spot where hundreds of species pass by.
