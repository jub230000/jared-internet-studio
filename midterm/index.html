Where wonder meets reality!

Say hello to the famous "Playa Escondida"! (Hidden Beach) Located in the Marieta Islands off the coast of Puerto Vallarta, this sandy cavern was formed by volcanic activity and is home to many different coral, reef fish, sea turtles, dolphins, manta rays, and eels! During the winter, humpback whales pass near the shores!


Visit Us!

Nayarit is one of the 31 states within Mexico. The state covers 27,815 square kilometers and has very nutrient dense soil thanks in part to its 2 volcanoes. Nayarit is right next to Jalisco and is right on the west coastline of Mexico and has many islands. Famous beaches are located here aswell including Puerto Vallarta, Guayabitos, and San Blas! This area is also rich in indigenous culture and influence, as it is home to the Huichol and Cora people. It is also home to many form of wildlife including the golden eagle, coyotes, coral snakes, lilac-crowned parrots, and many many more! So come visit our beautiful state sometime soon and witness rich cultural history and landmarks!
