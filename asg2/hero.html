About

Lebron James was born December 30th 1984. He is an American professional basketball player with many achievements to his name. As of today (September 3rd, 2025) he plays for the Los Angeles Lakers as a power forward. He currently has won 4 NBA championships

Achievements

Lebron currently has 4 NBA chapionship rings. He also holds the NBA record of all time points. (42,184) He ranks 4th in NBA career assists and 6th in NBA career steals. He also has 4 NBA MVP awards, 4 NBA finals award, rooke of the year award, 3 All-Star game MVP awards, an NBA cup MVP award, an Olympic gold medal, and the Olympics MVP award.
