Denise Jacobs argues that we all have a troll-like inner critic. Of the methods she identifies to banish this troll, which would be most successful for you? Why?

I think the method that would work best for me is to remold my brain. I already do this alot to help me cope with daily issues and situations. For example, I often will think to myself "What is the worst that can happen if ____?". Remolding my brain would help me to change my mindset on any situation or project I am working on.

In David McRaney's article, what is "hyperbolic discounting"? List one method McRaney identifies to counteract hyperbolic discounting. Which would work best for you?

One method McRaney indentifies to counteract hyperbolic discounting is to buy a planner and write notes. This involves you keeping track of your tasks, responsibilites, and ideas. This a method I am currently utilizing and so far it has worked wonderfully. Having a physical planner to remind me of what I need to do on what day of the week is such a huge help and encourages me to stay ahead.
