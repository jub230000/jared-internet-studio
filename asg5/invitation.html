Guests of Honor

Guests

Conan O'Brien

Conan is my absolute favorite TV show host. He is far more energetic and charismatic than any of the other late night show hosts. I also really enjoyed his HBO Max original where he traveled the world to visit his fans. There is a video of him giving time and respect to a lady in Palestine who was explaining her views on the Israel/Palestine conflict.

Hayley Williams

Easy pick. Hayley Williams is a generational talent and absolutely deserves all the credit she gets. Funny enough, I recently found out that she is also featured in "Seein' Stars" by Turnstile (One of my favorite Turnstile songs) as a background vocal. Super iconic, outspoken, and inspirational person.

Dak Prescott

Most overhated quarterback in the NFL. I used to feed into the hate people throw at this guy, but now I am way over that. Dak does has his screw-ups, but he is a great quarterback. Especially this season with our high powered offense. Just wish the Cowboys had a defense.
