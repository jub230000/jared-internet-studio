More About Thundercat

Thundercat is an American producer/bassist/artist born October 19, 1984. He is most known for his solo projects involving his ridiculous 6 string bass. He also played with the iconic Suicidal Tendencies for a little while before going solo with his R&B/funk. (It is hard to define his music, you would have to listen to it yourself)
